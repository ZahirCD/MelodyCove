    • Welcome to MelodyCove's Become an Artist Page! You must fulfill these criterias to register as an Artist!

    1. Play Count: Your uploads must collectively reach a minimum of 500,000 plays. This criterion reflects the engagement and interest your music has generated from the Audiomack community.

    2. Follower Count: Your profile should have at least 1,000 followers. This showcases your reach and influence among listeners on the platform.

    3. Complete Profile: Your artist profile should be up-to-date with a clear photo, a well-written bio that introduces you and your musical style, and links to your active social media accounts.

    4. Social Media Presence: Providing links to your official social media profiles as much as possible enhances your credibility and reinforces your digital footprint.

    5. Official Product Releases: You must have a minimum of two (2) original product releases. These releases should be your original compositions and not include covers, remixes, or similar content.
